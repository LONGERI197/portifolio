FARM WITH BISASE AFRICA

About Us

Farm with Bisase Africa is an organization started in 2019 to share Agriculture, Food Security and Rural Development stories on the online Platform. We believe Africa will attain its glory through Agriculture as its underutilized. Our is to cover stories on Agriculture across the continent that will change the perspectives about Agriculture.

What we do

  1. Production of Arrowroots, Irish Potatoes and Vegetables
  2. Marketing of Agricultural Produce
  3. Training and Mentorship in the Agriculture Space

AGRIBONGA STORIES

AGRIBONGA Series is a monthly program where we share agriprenuer journey/stories to educate , encourage and promote partnerships in the different agriculture spaces from production, postharvest management, value addition and underutilized opportunities in the Agriculture space.

Some of the stories weve covered are:

  1. Career As an Agronomist
  2. Mushroom Farming
  3. Irish Potato Aggregation
  4. Chicken Farming
  5. Cobriq Bricol Enterprises
